Keanu Reeves

The beloved actor with a heart of gold

Timeline of Keanu Reeves' Life

  • 1964 - Born in Beirut, Lebanon
  • 1970s - Moves to Canada with his mother and siblings after his parents' divorce, growing up in Toronto
  • 1984 - Begins acting in TV and film, with early roles in Canadian productions
  • 1986 - Breakthrough role in River's Edge, gaining attention for his unique, introspective acting style
  • 1989 - Stars as Ted in Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure, achieving mainstream success and becoming a cultural icon
  • 1991 - Continues success with Point Break, showcasing his versatility in action films
  • 1994 - Takes on the lead role in Speed, catapulting him to international fame as an action star
  • 1999 - Stars as Neo in The Matrix, redefining science fiction and action cinema, with groundbreaking special effects and a compelling storyline
  • 2005 - Stars in Constantine, further diversifying his filmography with supernatural thrillers
  • 2014 - Returns to action with John Wick, a role that revitalizes his career and becomes a cult favorite, leading to multiple sequels
  • 2019 - Gains online popularity as a beloved, down-to-earth celebrity with memorable moments at conventions and in interviews; known for kindness, humility, and gratitude toward fans
  • 2021 - Works on The Matrix Resurrections, reuniting with the world-renowned Matrix franchise
